Welcome to the world of game engines

Why do you study

Game engine is the jewel of the game's crown.

The development of a game engine involves a wide range of knowledge. And even if you do not work this in the future, it's still valuable to learn.

Through it, you will know how humans compress the world we live in into the engine.
